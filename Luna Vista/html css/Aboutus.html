About Us
Luna Vista Resort began as a family-owned lakeside retreat decades ago, built on a passion for warm hospitality and unforgettable sunsets. Over the years it has grown into a luxurious escape where modern comfort meets natural beauty—offering elegant rooms, world-class dining, and curated experiences that celebrate the region’s culture and calm.
  • Comfortable, beautifully styled rooms with scenic views
  • Locally-inspired dining experiences crafted from fresh ingredients
  • Curated activities that connect you to the surrounding nature
  • Warm service rooted in family tradition
Today, Luna Vista invites guests to relax by the water, explore nearby trails, and create lasting memories in a place that feels both timeless and refreshingly new.
